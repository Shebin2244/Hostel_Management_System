Hostel Rules and Regulations

Rule 1: Quiet Hours

Quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM. Please keep noise levels to a minimum during these hours.

Rule 2: Smoking

Smoking is strictly prohibited inside the hostel building.

Rule 3: Guests

Guests are not allowed in the hostel rooms without prior permission from the warden.

Rule 4: Cleanliness and Hygiene

Proper cleanliness and hygiene must be maintained in the common areas and individual rooms.

Failure to follow these rules may result in disciplinary actions. If you have any questions or need clarification on any of the rules, please contact the hostel management.
